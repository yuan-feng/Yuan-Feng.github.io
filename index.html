About

My name is Yuan Feng. I am a Ph.D. candidate working in the Computational Mechanics Group at University of California, Davis. I completed my bachelors with major in Civil Engineering at Beijing Jiaotong University.

My research lies in the numerical methods and modeling of engineering problems. My research focus is on the study of inelastic effects in soil-foundation-structure interaction systems subject to seismic loading conditions. I am working on the development of inelastic models to correctly model the soil behaviors. The majority of the work is related to the development, verification, and application in the Real ESSI Simulator System.
